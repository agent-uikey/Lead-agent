# Lead-agent

**Lead-agent** is an AI-powered assistant for business research, market analysis, and lead generation. It helps entrepreneurs, startups, and businesses with in-depth insights on starting, positioning, and scaling their ventures.

## Features

- **Market Research:** Deep-dive analysis of industry trends, market size, and competitive landscape for any business vertical.
- **Business Strategy:** Step-by-step guidance on product development, manufacturing, regulatory compliance, and go-to-market strategy.
- **Lead Generation:** Identify target customers, distribution channels, and partnership opportunities.
- **Scaling Playbooks:** Social media growth, D2C ecommerce, marketplace listings, and omnichannel distribution strategies.
- **Industry Expertise:** Covers sectors including consumer goods, cosmetics, fragrance, food & beverage, technology, and more.

## Example Use Cases

- Conducting market research for a new sunscreen brand in India.
- Building a launch roadmap for a D2C fragrance company.
- Identifying key competitors, pricing tiers, and consumer trends in any market segment.
- Drafting regulatory compliance checklists for cosmetics (CDSCO, FDA, EU).
- Developing a social media and performance marketing strategy.
